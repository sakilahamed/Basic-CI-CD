Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about my services

What types of penetration testing do you offer?

I offer comprehensive penetration testing services including network penetration testing, web application security testing, wireless security assessment, and social engineering assessments. Each test is tailored to your specific infrastructure and requirements.

How long does a typical security assessment take?

The duration depends on the scope and complexity of your environment. A typical web application penetration test takes 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive network assessments may take 2-4 weeks. I provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

Do you provide reports and remediation guidance?

Yes, all assessments include comprehensive reports with executive summaries, technical findings, risk ratings, and detailed remediation guidance. I also provide follow-up consultations to help implement the recommendations.

Can you help with compliance requirements?

Absolutely! I have experience with various compliance frameworks including PCI DSS, HIPAA, SOX, and ISO 27001. I can help assess your current compliance status and develop strategies to meet regulatory requirements.

Do you offer emergency incident response services?

Yes, I provide 24/7 emergency incident response services for active security breaches. I can help contain threats, conduct forensic analysis, and develop recovery strategies to minimize business impact.

What makes your approach different?

I combine advanced technical skills with practical business understanding. My approach focuses on real-world attack scenarios and provides actionable recommendations that align with your business objectives and budget constraints.
